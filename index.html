About Jogoo CBO

Who We Are

Jogoo Community Based Organization, established on February 18th, 2020, is based in Ukunda, Kona ya Chifu, adjacent to the Sub-chief's office in Msambweni constituency, Kwale County, Kenya.

Our Vision

To empower youth and young people to embrace the scout promise and apply it in their daily lives effectively.

Our Mission

To promote Scout Craft, Scout Law, Scout Motto, and Scout Spirit globally, upholding the principle of "Once a scout, always a scout."

Our Objectives

  • Provide services for youth and scouts in Kenya, fostering global scout brotherhood.
  • Enhance scout craft and integrate scout law into youth development.
  • Unite scouts at sub-county, county, national, and global levels.
  • Serve as a bridge between scout leaders and units.
  • Promote environmental conservation initiatives.
  • Empower young people to build a better world.

Our Activities

Jogoo CBO runs impactful activities focused on scouting, youth empowerment, and community development in Kenya.

Community Projects

We implement community-driven projects in Ukunda to address local needs and promote sustainable development.
