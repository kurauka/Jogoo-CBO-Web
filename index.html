About Jogoo CBO

Who We Are

Jogoo Community Based Organization was formed on February 18th, 2020. We are located in Ukunda, Kona ya Chifu, adjacent to the Sub-chief's office in Msambweni constituency, Kwale County, Kenya.

Our Vision

To empower youth and young people to discover and work effectively with the scout promise and apply them in their day-to-day life.

Our Mission

To facilitate the extension of Scout Craft, Scout Law, Scout Motto, and Scout Spirit in the world of scouting, bearing in mind the principle of "Once a scout always a scout".

Our Objectives

  • Provide services for Youth and Scouts in Kenya and create brotherhood in worldwide scouts
  • Enhance scout craft and provide useful ideas to facilitate scout law in youth's life
  • Unite scouts at sub-County, County, National, and Global levels
  • Be the link of scouting between leaders and Scout units
  • Conserve the environment
  • Empower young people to create a better world
